✦VISIT GUARDA✦

Enjoy the outdoors, culture and leizure that the highest city of Portugal has to offer.

Top five activities to do at Guarda

Cathedral

Visit one of the most imponent catholic monuments.

Serra da Estrela

Enjoy the view of the highest mountain of the country. During the Winter, you can ski, snowboard or simply build a snowman!

River Beaches

Have fun at the beautiful river beaches, where you can refresh, relax and sunbathe.

Mondego Walkways

Have a walk along the path that runs along River Mondego, placed at the heart of Serra da Estrela Natural Park and UNESCO Estrela Geopark.

Theater

Attend a show or a concert at a modern and contemporary place.
